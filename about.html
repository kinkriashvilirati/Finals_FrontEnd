About NOVA

Shopping should feel clear, useful, and human.

We bring together practical products for home and everyday life in a store designed to be easy to use.

N

Our idea

Less noise. More useful finds.

NOVA Market is a fictional general store created as a frontend course project. Its purpose is to demonstrate a complete, responsive shopping experience with clean and understandable code.

Every part of the site—from the product catalogue to the registration form—has been built using semantic HTML, modern CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.
